Help Build This Resource

This guide is a living document. The best resources often come from clinicians discovering what works in their own practice. If you've found a helpful article, a prompt that saves you time, or a tool worth knowing about, share it here.

What We're Looking For
  • Links: Articles, papers, videos, or tools that helped you understand or use AI
  • Prompts: Effective prompts you've developed for clinical tasks
  • Tips: Practical advice from your experience using AI in healthcare

Submit a Resource

We won't share your email or add you to any lists.
Helps us categorize and surface your contribution to the right audience.

Guidelines

For Links

  • Prefer primary sources (original research, official documentation) over summaries
  • Check that the link works and isn't behind a hard paywall
  • Briefly explain what makes it valuable and who would benefit

For Prompts

  • Include the full prompt text so others can use it directly
  • Note which AI tool(s) you've tested it with
  • Describe the clinical task it helps with
  • Never include PHI—use generic placeholders like [patient age], [medication], etc.

For Tips

  • Be specific about what worked and in what context
  • Share what you learned, including what didn't work
  • Consider what others in similar situations would need to know
Privacy Reminder

Never include protected health information (PHI) in any submission. All prompts and examples should use de-identified, hypothetical scenarios only.
